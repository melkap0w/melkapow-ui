Intro

I paint creation. If you look at your hands, you see the flow of life. There are different sizes and shapes of every single line marking unique individuality. They are the building blocks of life, but in reality this is everything that makes up you. Painted in color because that's where you belong.

Dive deep inward into a world that is uncaptured. Notice what may flow and what resists, what raw depth brings forward expressed in simple colors.
