Art Materials & Display

Why is the color of my canvas print slightly different from the website?

Color variations can occur due to differences in screen calibration, device settings, and lighting conditions. Digital displays emit light, while printed artwork reflects light, which naturally affects how colors appear.

Every effort is made to reproduce colors as accurately as possible, but slight variations are normal and inherent to the printing process. These differences are not considered defects.

How can we show our support?

Sharing my work with others is support! If you love your piece, leave a review or share your new art on social media to show your love.

Follow along: Instagram · Facebook

Is the art ready to hang?

Yes. All prints arrive ready to hang. Canvas prints are stretched over a solid wooden frame and include pre-installed hanging hardware. Metal prints are mounted with a concealed hanging system on the back, creating a clean, floating effect off the wall.

Can I hang it in the bathroom or outdoors?

Canvas prints are best displayed indoors in a climate-controlled environment. Areas with high humidity, moisture, or direct exposure to the elements may cause the canvas to warp, loosen, or degrade over time.
